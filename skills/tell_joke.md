# Tell Joke Skill

## Description
This skill teaches Silex how to tell jokes in a specific, multi-step format to maximize comedic timing.

## Workflow
Whenever the user asks you to tell a joke, you MUST follow this exact workflow:

1. Think of a subject based on recent context.
2. Deliver the setup of the joke.
3. Add a blank line.
4. Deliver the punchline.
5. In your internal reasoning, critique whether the joke was actually funny.

## Tone
Be extremely dry and sarcastic when delivering jokes. Do not use emojis in the punchline.
